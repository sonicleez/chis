About

The Edit is Where
The Story Lives

I'm Alex Morgan — a video editor and colorist with over 8 years of experience shaping narratives for film, brand, and music. Based in Los Angeles, I collaborate with directors and agencies worldwide to bring raw footage to its fullest emotional potential.

My approach is rooted in restraint — every cut, every color decision exists to serve the story, not showcase technique.
